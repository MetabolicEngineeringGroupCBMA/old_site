This medium is for general growth of E. coli:

10 g/L Tryptone or Peptone
 5 g/L yeast extract
10 g/L NaCl
(20 g/L agar)

pH usually 7 using tapwater

This medium can be premade in dry form and then prepared using a microwave oven: [LBpowder]
