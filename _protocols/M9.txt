PROTOCOL[EXP:true]
5x M9 Minimal Media 
per liter
30 g       Na2HPO4
15 g       KH2PO4
5 g        NH4Cl
2.5 g      NaCl
15 mg      CaCl2 (optional) 
Use tap water, which includes many trace metal ions, to bring to 1 liter. 

Autoclave for 20 minutes at 15 PSI. 
Always cool media to 50 deg C before adding antibiotics or vitamins. 
Before use add the following to 1 liter of 1x M9.

1 ml       1 M MgSO4 (Filter)
10 ml      20% Carbon source (either glucose/sugar or glycerol) (Autoclave separately)
0.1 ml     0.5% Thiamine, Vitamin B1 (Filter)

Adding sugar prior to autoclaving will cause sugar to turn brown or black. 
Sugar or glycerol stock solution can be autoclaved or filter sterilized separately.
